Project Title:
Smart Online Clothing Store Management System.


Project Overview:
A full-featured web application designed to manage an online clothing store with two main user roles:
Client (Customer Side)
Seller (Admin Side)
The system allows customers to browse and purchase clothing items, while sellers manage products, orders, and customer interactions efficiently.
